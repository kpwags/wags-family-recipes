## Ingredients

- 2 oz. Vodka
- 1 oz. Triple Sec Orange Liquor or Blood Orange Liquor
- 0.5 oz. Simple Syrup
- 2 oz. Orange Juice

## Directions

1. Pour ingredients in shaker over ice. Shake and pour into glass.
